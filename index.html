About BIG Logistic

BIG Logistic is a leading global logistics and supply chain management company, established with a vision to revolutionize the way businesses move their goods across the world. With over two decades of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for reliability, efficiency, and innovation in logistics solutions.

Our comprehensive network spans across 150+ countries, enabling us to provide seamless end-to-end logistics services including freight forwarding, warehousing, distribution, and supply chain consulting. We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional customer service and cutting-edge technology solutions.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, reliable, and cost-effective logistics solutions that empower businesses to reach their full potential in the global marketplace.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted and innovative logistics partner, connecting businesses and communities through seamless global supply chain solutions.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, and Customer-centricity form the foundation of everything we do at BIG Logistic.

About BIG Logistic - Detailed Overview

Founded in 2001, BIG Logistic has grown from a small regional carrier to one of the world's leading logistics companies. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make global trade more accessible and efficient for businesses of all sizes.

Today, we operate a fleet of over 10,000 vehicles, manage 200+ warehouses worldwide, and employ more than 50,000 dedicated professionals. Our advanced technology platform processes over 1 million shipments monthly, ensuring real-time tracking and transparency for our clients.

Key achievements include:

  • ISO 9001:2015 certified quality management system
  • Fair
  • 99.7% on-time delivery rate globally
  • 24/7 multilingual customer support in 40+ languages
  • Strategic partnerships with major airlines and shipping lines

Our Mission - Detailed

Our mission extends beyond simple transportation. We are committed to:

  • Innovation: Continuously developing cutting-edge logistics technologies and processes
  • Reliability: Ensuring consistent, dependable service that our clients can count on
  • Cost-effectiveness: Optimizing routes and processes to provide competitive pricing
  • Sustainability: Implementing eco-friendly practices to reduce environmental impact
  • Global Reach: Expanding our network to serve emerging markets and trade routes

We believe that by empowering businesses with superior logistics solutions, we contribute to global economic growth and prosperity. Every shipment we handle represents someone's dream, business goal, or essential need.

Our Vision - Detailed

Our vision for the future encompasses:

  • Digital Transformation: Leading the industry in AI-powered logistics optimization
  • Sustainable Operations: Achieving carbon neutrality across all operations by 2030
  • Global Connectivity: Bridging markets and enabling seamless international commerce
  • Community Impact: Supporting local communities through job creation and economic development
  • Customer Excellence: Setting new standards for service quality and customer satisfaction

We envision a world where distance is no barrier to business success, where small companies can compete globally, and where logistics operations contribute positively to environmental sustainability.

By 2030, we aim to be the first choice for businesses seeking logistics partners who understand that moving goods is about moving dreams, opportunities, and progress forward.
